<p><p>The absorbable patch market is primarily categorized into three types: PGA patches, trimethylene carbonate patches, and other patches. PGA patches, made from polyglycolic acid, are known for their strong tensile strength and rapid absorption. Trimethylene carbonate patches offer flexibility and are biocompatible, making them suitable for softer tissue integration. Other patches encompass various biodegradable polymers that cater to specific medical applications, such as wound healing and surgical repairs, enhancing patient outcomes through effective tissue support and regeneration.</p></p>
