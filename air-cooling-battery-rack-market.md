<p><p>The Air Cooling Battery Rack market features two primary types: passive air cooling and active air cooling. Passive air cooling relies on natural convection and ambient airflow to dissipate heat, making it energy-efficient and low-maintenance. In contrast, active air cooling employs fans or temperature control systems to enhance airflow and regulate battery temperatures, ensuring optimal performance in demanding environments. Both types are crucial for prolonging battery life and maintaining efficiency in energy storage and electric vehicle applications.</p></p>
