<p><p>The airbag wiring loom market can be categorized into three main types: H-shaped, E-shaped, and T-shaped configurations. H-shaped looms feature a central connector with arms extending outward, facilitating efficient connection to multiple airbag systems. E-shaped looms provide a compact design, ideal for tight spaces in vehicles while ensuring reliable connectivity. T-shaped looms exhibit a branching structure, allowing flexible routing around components and enhancing installation ease. Each design type caters to specific vehicle layouts and safety requirements, ensuring effective airbag deployment.</p></p>
