<p><p>The airport tugs market consists of two primary types: conventional towbars and towbarless tugs. Conventional towbars are traditional vehicles that connect to aircraft using a towbar, allowing for towing and maneuvering during ground operations. In contrast, towbarless tugs employ innovative designs that lift the aircraft without a physical connection, offering enhanced maneuverability and reducing aircraft wear and tear. Both types cater to different operational needs, with users considering factors like efficiency, safety, and ease of use for their ground handling requirements.</p></p>
