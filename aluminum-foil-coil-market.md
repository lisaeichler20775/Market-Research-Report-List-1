<p><p>The aluminum foil coil market comprises three primary types: hard foil, semi-hard foil, and soft foil. Hard foil is characterized by its rigidity and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring durability. Semi-hard foil offers a balance between flexibility and strength, ideal for packaging and insulation. Soft foil, being the most malleable, is commonly used for everyday applications like food wrapping and flexible packaging. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and construction.</p></p>
