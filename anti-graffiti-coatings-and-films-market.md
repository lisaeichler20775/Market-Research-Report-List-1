<p><ul><li>Non-Permanent</li><li>Permanent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-graffiti coatings and films are categorized into non-permanent and permanent types. Non-permanent solutions, like sacrificial coatings, allow for easy removal of graffiti by simply stripping the coating away. These options are cost-effective and ideal for areas with frequent vandalism. In contrast, permanent coatings create a strong, protective surface that resists graffiti without the need for frequent replacements. They are more durable and suitable for long-term protection on high-maintenance surfaces, providing lasting aesthetic benefits and reduced cleaning efforts.</p></p>
