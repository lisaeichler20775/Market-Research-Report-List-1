<p><p>Between bearing pumps are specialized centrifugal pumps designed primarily for heavy-duty applications where high efficiency and reliability are crucial. These pumps are characterized by their unique configuration, with the impeller positioned between two bearings, which helps in balancing the hydraulic forces and reducing vibration. This design enhances their performance in moving viscous fluids and accommodating high flow rates, making them ideal for industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and water treatment.</p><p>The Between Bearing Pumps Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 14.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand across various sectors.
