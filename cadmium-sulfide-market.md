<p><p>The Cadmium Sulfide (CdS) market is segmented into Alpha and Beta types based on crystal structure. Alpha-type CdS has a hexagonal structure, offering higher thermal stability and is commonly used in photovoltaic cells and sensors. Beta-type CdS has a cubic structure, providing better optical properties, making it suitable for optoelectronic devices like LEDs and lasers. Both types are essential in electronics, but their distinct properties cater to specific industrial applications, influencing market demand and pricing.</p></p>
