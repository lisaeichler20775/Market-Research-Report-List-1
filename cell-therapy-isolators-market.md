<p><p>The cell therapy isolators market comprises various types, notably multi-chamber and double-chamber isolators. Multi-chamber isolators allow for simultaneous processing of multiple samples in distinct compartments, enhancing efficiency and reducing contamination risk. Double-chamber isolators, on the other hand, consist of two separate chambers that facilitate different processes, such as material transfer and cell manipulation, while maintaining strict aseptic conditions. Both designs are crucial in ensuring sterility and safety in cell therapy applications, catering to the growing demand for innovative therapeutic solutions.</p></p>
