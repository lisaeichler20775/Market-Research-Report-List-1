The market is segmented into Negatively Charged N Series and Positively Charged P Series. The N Series enhances properties such as conductivity and anti-static characteristics, making it suitable for applications needing static control. In contrast, the P Series improves adhesion and wettability, optimizing performance in coatings and films. Both series cater to specific industrial needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
