<p><p>The Chip-on-Board (CoB) LED market consists of three main types: Lateral Chip, Vertical Chip, and Flip Chip. Lateral Chip configuration has the LED chip positioned horizontally, ideal for applications requiring wider light distribution. Vertical Chip features a vertically aligned LED, enhancing thermal management and compactness for high-density applications. Flip Chip technology places the LED chip upside down, enabling improved efficiency and better light extraction. Each type caters to specific lighting needs in industrial, commercial, and residential sectors.</p></p>
