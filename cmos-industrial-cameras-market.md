<p><p>CMOS industrial cameras are categorized primarily into two types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture complete images at once, making them ideal for applications that require high-resolution still images, such as quality inspection and object recognition. In contrast, line scan cameras capture one line of the image at a time, suited for scenarios like web inspection and packaging, where continuous motion is involved. Both types leverage CMOS technology for enhanced performance and efficiency.</p></p>
