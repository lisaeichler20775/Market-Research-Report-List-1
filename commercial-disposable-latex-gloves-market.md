<p><p>The commercial disposable latex gloves market is divided into two main types: powdered and no-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a cornstarch or talcum powder, which facilitates easier donning and doffing, but may cause allergic reactions in some users. No-powdered gloves, on the other hand, are free from powders, reducing the potential for irritation and allergies, making them preferable in sensitive environments. Both types cater to various industries, including healthcare, food service, and cleaning, depending on specific user needs.</p></p>
