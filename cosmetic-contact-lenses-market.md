<p><p>The cosmetic contact lenses market comprises three primary types: rigid, soft, and hybrid lenses. Rigid contact lenses are durable and offer excellent vision correction but may take time to adapt to. Soft contact lenses are popular for their comfort and ease of use, often available in various designs and colors for cosmetic enhancement. Hybrid contact lenses combine the best features of both, featuring a rigid center for sharp vision surrounded by a soft outer layer for comfort, appealing to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
