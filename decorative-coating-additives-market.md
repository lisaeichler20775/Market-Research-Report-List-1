<p><p>The decorative coating additives market comprises various systems, including water-based, solvent-based, and powder-based formulations. Water-based systems utilize water as a primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions and ease of cleanup. Solvent-based systems rely on organic solvents, providing enhanced durability and resistance but often with higher environmental impact. Powder-based systems involve dry powder coatings that require heat for application, offering benefits like reduced waste and emissions. Other market types include gel and hybrid systems, catering to diverse application needs and preferences.</p></p>
