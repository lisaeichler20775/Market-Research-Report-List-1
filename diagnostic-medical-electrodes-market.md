<p><ul><li>Wet Electrodes</li><li>Dry Electrodes</li><li>Needle Electrodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diagnostic Medical Electrodes market is categorized into wet, dry, and needle electrodes. Wet electrodes are coated with a conductive gel or electrolyte, enhancing signal quality for procedures like ECG and EEG. Dry electrodes eliminate the need for gels, offering convenience and reduced skin irritation, making them suitable for continuous monitoring. Needle electrodes involve inserting thin needles into the skin for precise measurements, commonly used in electromyography to assess muscle activity. Each type serves specific diagnostic purposes in medical settings.</p></p>
