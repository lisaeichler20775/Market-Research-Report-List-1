<p><p>The disposable exam gloves market primarily consists of three main types: latex, rubber, and nitrile. Latex gloves, made from natural rubber, offer excellent dexterity and comfort but can cause allergic reactions in some users. Rubber gloves, typically more resilient and durable, are often used in general-purpose applications. Nitrile gloves, crafted from synthetic materials, are latex-free, providing strong chemical resistance and puncture protection, making them ideal for medical and industrial environments. Each type caters to specific user needs and preferences.</p></p>
