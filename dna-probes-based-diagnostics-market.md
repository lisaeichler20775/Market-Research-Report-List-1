<p><strong>The DNA Probes Based Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Genomic Probe</li><li>cDNA Probe</li><li>Oligonucleotide Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA probes-based diagnostics consist of various types categorized by their specific applications. Genomic probes target specific DNA sequences within the genome, useful for genetic mapping and diagnosis of hereditary diseases. cDNA probes, derived from complementary DNA, are primarily used for gene expression studies, helping to identify active genes in specific tissues. Oligonucleotide probes are short, synthetic sequences that bind to complementary DNA or RNA, facilitating the detection and quantification of nucleic acids in various diagnostic assays. Each type plays a crucial role in molecular diagnostics.</p></p>
