<p><p>The E-Scooter Sharing market is primarily categorized into two types: dockless and station-based systems. Dockless e-scooter sharing allows users to unlock scooters via a mobile app and park them anywhere within a designated zone, offering flexibility and convenience. In contrast, station-based systems require users to pick up and drop off e-scooters at designated docking stations, providing more control over the fleet's distribution and availability. Both models aim to promote eco-friendly transportation solutions while catering to different user preferences and urban landscapes.</p></p>
