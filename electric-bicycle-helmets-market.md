<p><strong>The Electric Bicycle Helmets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Face Helmet</li><li>Open Face Helmet</li><li>Half Helmet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bicycle helmets market comprises three main types: full face helmets, open face helmets, and half helmets. Full face helmets provide maximum protection by covering the entire head, including the chin and face, ideal for high-speed riding. Open face helmets offer coverage for the head and back of the neck while leaving the face exposed, balancing safety and visibility. Half helmets cover only the top of the head, providing minimal protection and a lightweight option for casual riders. Each type caters to different rider preferences and safety needs.</p></p>
