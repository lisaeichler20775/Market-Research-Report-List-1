<p><ul><li>Buck Converter</li><li>Boost Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle DC-DC converter market primarily includes buck and boost converters. Buck converters lower voltage from a higher level to provide efficient power to components such as motors and batteries. They ensure optimal energy usage and extend battery life. Conversely, boost converters increase voltage from a lower level, enabling efficient charging of auxiliary systems or batteries requiring higher voltage levels. Together, these converters play a critical role in managing power distribution within electric vehicles, enhancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
