<p><p>The electronic fan market comprises three main types: axially and centrifugal, along with other fan variants. Axial electronic fans move air parallel to the fan axis, making them efficient for cooling in compact spaces. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, utilize a rotating impeller to draw air in and discharge it at a right angle, providing higher pressure and airflow for larger applications. Other market types may include mixed-flow fans or specialized designs tailored for specific industrial needs.</p></p>
