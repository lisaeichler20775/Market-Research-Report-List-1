<p><strong>The Fiberglass Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grinding</li><li>Incineration</li><li>Pyrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass recycling market includes various methods to manage waste fiberglass efficiently. Grinding involves mechanically reducing fiberglass into smaller particles, which can be repurposed in construction or composite materials. Incineration, on the other hand, entails burning fiberglass waste at high temperatures, converting it into energy while reducing landfill volume. Pyrolysis is a thermal decomposition process that breaks down fiberglass into its elemental components, allowing for the recovery of useful materials and fuels. Each method aims to enhance sustainability and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
