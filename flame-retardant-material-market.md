<p><p>The flame retardant material market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic flame retardant materials. Organic flame retardants are derived from carbon-based compounds, often used for their effectiveness and versatility in polymers and textiles. In contrast, inorganic flame retardants are mineral-based, commonly containing elements like phosphorus, boron, and magnesium, known for their thermal stability and low toxicity. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing fire resistance in various applications, ensuring safety in industries such as construction and automotive.</p></p>
