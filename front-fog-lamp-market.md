<p><ul><li>LED</li><li>Xenon</li><li>Halogen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The front fog lamp market is segmented into three main types: LED, Xenon, and Halogen. LED fog lamps are energy-efficient, have a long lifespan, and provide bright illumination while consuming less power. Xenon lights offer high-intensity illumination, ideal for adverse weather conditions, but usually come at a higher cost. Halogen fog lamps are the most traditional option, providing adequate brightness at a lower cost, though they consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs and Xenons.</p></p>
