<p><p>The functional sugar market encompasses various types, including oligosaccharides, inulin, sugar alcohols, and others. Oligosaccharides are prebiotic fibers that support gut health, while inulin, a soluble fiber, aids digestion and enhances food texture. Sugar alcohols, such as xylitol and erythritol, provide sweetening with fewer calories and lower glycemic impact, making them popular in low-sugar products. Other functional sugars may include rare sugars or naturally derived sweeteners, catering to health-conscious consumers and driving innovations in food and beverage formulations.</p></p>
