<p><p>The Graphene Nano Platelets market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Layer Graphene Nanosheets and Bilayer Graphene Nanosheets. Single-layer graphene nanosheets consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a 2D hexagonal lattice, offering exceptional electrical, thermal, and mechanical properties suitable for various applications, including electronics and composites. Bilayer graphene nanosheets, made of two stacked graphene layers, exhibit enhanced strength and electrical conductivity, making them ideal for advanced materials, sensors, and energy storage solutions.</p></p>
