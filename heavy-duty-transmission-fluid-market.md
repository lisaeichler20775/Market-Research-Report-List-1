<p><p>The heavy-duty transmission fluid market consists of three primary types: Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF), designed for manual gearboxes to ensure smooth shifting and protect against wear; Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF), formulated for automatic transmissions to maintain proper function, lubricate components, and manage heat; and Automated Manual Transmission (AMT) Fluid, a blend of manual and automatic characteristics, tailored for vehicles with AMT systems, which combine manual gear shifting with automatic operation for enhanced fuel efficiency and performance.</p></p>
