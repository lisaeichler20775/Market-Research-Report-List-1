<p><p>The HJT texturing equipment market comprises two main types: acid texturing equipment and alkaline texturing equipment. Acid texturing equipment utilizes acidic solutions to create micro-rough surface profiles on silicon wafers, enhancing light absorption and photovoltaic efficiency. In contrast, alkaline texturing equipment employs alkaline solutions, offering an alternative method for achieving similar surface characteristics with potentially reduced environmental impact. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing the performance of Heterojunction Technology (HJT) solar cells by improving light-trapping capabilities.</p></p>
