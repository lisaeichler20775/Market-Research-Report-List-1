<p><strong>The Hydraulic Spreader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Single Acting"</li><li>"Double Acting"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic spreader market is divided into two main types: single acting and double acting. Single acting hydraulic spreaders operate using fluid pressure in one direction, allowing for quick expansion and requiring a return mechanism to retract. In contrast, double acting spreaders utilize fluid pressure for both expansion and retraction, providing more precise control and faster operation. These distinctions cater to various applications in construction, rescue operations, and industrial settings, influencing equipment efficiency and user preference.</p></p>
