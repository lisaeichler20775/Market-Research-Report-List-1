<p><p>The hygienic hand dryer market is primarily divided into two types: jet hygienic hand dryers and hot hygienic hand dryers. Jet hygienic hand dryers use high-speed, focused air jets to quickly remove moisture from hands, promoting faster drying times and often enhancing hygiene by minimizing touch points. In contrast, hot hygienic hand dryers use warm air to evaporate water on the skin, providing a more comforting experience but typically requiring a longer drying duration. Both types aim to reduce paper waste and improve hygiene in public restrooms.</p></p>
