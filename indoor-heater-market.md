<p><p>The indoor heater market comprises several types, including radiative space heaters, which emit infrared radiation to warm objects and people directly. Convective space heaters warm the air, circulating heat throughout the room. Filler space heaters, often oil-filled, provide consistent warmth and can retain heat for longer periods. Other heaters may include ceramic, fan-forced, and electric baseboard units, each offering unique advantages in terms of efficiency, heat distribution, and design, catering to various consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
