<p><p>Industrial thermocouples are temperature sensors used for measuring high temperatures across various industrial applications, such as manufacturing, energy, and HVAC systems. They consist of two dissimilar metal wires joined at one end, generating a voltage proportional to the temperature difference between the joined and open ends. Their robustness, wide temperature range, and cost-effectiveness make them preferred choices for temperature measurement in harsh industrial environments.</p><p>The Industrial Thermocouples Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.8% during the forecast period.
