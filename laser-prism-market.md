<p><strong>The Laser Prism Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Right Angle Prism</li><li>Wedge Prism</li><li>Penta Prism</li><li>Amici Roof Prism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser prism market comprises various types, each serving unique optical functions. Right angle prisms are utilized for reflecting light at precise angles, while wedge prisms alter the beam path and can introduce controlled dispersion. Penta prisms provide a 90-degree reflection, ensuring a consistent output direction, ideal for surveying instruments. Amici roof prisms invert images and are commonly used in binoculars and telescopes, facilitating clear viewing. Together, these prisms play crucial roles in enhancing optical systems across diverse applications.</p></p>
