<p><p>LiDAR, or Light Detection and Ranging, plays a critical role in the autonomous vehicle market, enabling precise mapping and obstacle detection. There are two primary types: solid-state LiDAR and mechanical/scanning LiDAR. Solid-state LiDAR consists of fixed components, offering compactness and durability, ideal for cost-effective applications. In contrast, mechanical/scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture a wider field of view, providing high-resolution 3D mapping but often at a higher cost and complexity. Both types enhance vehicle safety and navigation.</p></p>
