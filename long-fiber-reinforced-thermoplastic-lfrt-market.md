<p><p>Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (LFRT) is a type of composite material made by combining thermoplastic resins with long fibers, such as glass or carbon fibers. LFRT is widely used in various applications, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods, as it allows for more efficient manufacturing processes and offers design flexibility.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates that the LFRT market is experiencing significant expansion due to increasing demand for lightweight materials in automotive and aerospace industries aimed at improving fuel efficiency and performance.
