<p><p>The lung tumor embolization market encompasses various techniques for treating lung tumors. Transarterial chemoembolization (TACE) involves delivering chemotherapy directly to the tumor while blocking blood flow, enhancing drug effectiveness. Transarterial radioembolization (TARE) uses tiny radioactive beads to target tumors, providing localized radiation therapy. Other methods may include radiofrequency ablation or cryoablation, which destroy tumor cells through heat or extreme cold. Together, these approaches aim to improve patient outcomes by minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue.</p></p>
