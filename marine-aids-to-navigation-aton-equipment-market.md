<p><p>Marine Aids to Navigation (AtoN) equipment includes various types essential for safe maritime navigation. Buoys are floating markers that indicate channels and hazards. Fog signals provide sound alerts to vessels in low visibility conditions. Day beacons are stationary markers visible by day that guide maritime traffic. Lighthouses are tall structures that aid navigation with light signals, often marking dangerous coastlines. Additional categories, referred to as "Others," encompass specialized devices like electronic buoys and advanced positioning systems, enhancing navigational safety.</p></p>
