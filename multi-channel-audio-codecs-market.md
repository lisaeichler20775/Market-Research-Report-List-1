<p><p>The multi-channel audio codecs market comprises two main types: analog and digital. Analog codecs process audio signals in their continuous form, making them suitable for traditional audio systems and providing a warmer sound quality. Digital codecs, on the other hand, convert audio into a digital format for processing, offering versatility, enhanced compression, and support for advanced features like streaming and surround sound. Both types cater to different needs, enabling various applications ranging from consumer electronics to professional audio production.</p></p>
