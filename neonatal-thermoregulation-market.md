<p><strong>The Neonatal Thermoregulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neonatal Cooling Systems</li><li>Neonatal Incubators</li><li>Warmer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neonatal thermoregulation market comprises three primary types: neonatal cooling systems, neonatal incubators, and warmers. Neonatal cooling systems are designed to manage hyperthermia in newborns, effectively lowering body temperature to prevent brain damage. Neonatal incubators provide a controlled environment with regulated temperature and humidity, essential for premature or ill infants. Warmers, on the other hand, maintain an elevated temperature, offering immediate warmth for newborns at risk of hypothermia. Collectively, these technologies are vital for ensuring the optimal thermal environment for vulnerable newborns.</p></p>
