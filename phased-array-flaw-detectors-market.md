The linear array type features sensors arranged in a straight line, ideal for inspecting flat surfaces and welds, providing uniform coverage. In contrast, circular arrays consist of sensors arranged in a circular pattern, which allows for more versatile inspection of complex geometries and curved surfaces. Both types enhance detection capabilities and speed up inspections, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
