<p><strong>The Photoresist Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li><li>Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist cleaners market consists of two main types: positive photoresist strippers and negative photoresist strippers. Positive PR strippers are designed to remove positive photoresist materials, which become soluble upon exposure to light, facilitating precise patterning in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, negative PR strippers target negative photoresist materials, which harden when exposed to light, making them ideal for applications requiring enhanced durability. Both types of strippers are essential for maintaining cleanliness and accuracy in microfabrication processes.</p></p>
