<p><ul><li>Vacuum Pulp Washing Equipment</li><li>Pressure Pulp Washing Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulp washer market consists of two main types: vacuum pulp washing equipment and pressure pulp washing equipment. Vacuum pulp washing utilizes a vacuum to draw water through the pulp, effectively removing contaminants and improving pulp quality. This method is energy-efficient and enhances yield. In contrast, pressure pulp washing operates by using pressure to drive water through the pulp, facilitating a high-throughput process that can handle larger volumes. Both types are essential for optimizing pulp processing in the paper production industry.</p></p>
