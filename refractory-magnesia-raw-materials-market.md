<p><p>The refractory magnesia raw materials market comprises three main types: fused magnesia, dead burned magnesia, and others. Fused magnesia is produced by melting high-purity magnesite and is known for its excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties, making it ideal for high-temperature applications. Dead burned magnesia is created through calcining magnesite at high temperatures and is extensively used in refractory bricks and castables. The "others" category includes various specialized forms and blends that cater to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
