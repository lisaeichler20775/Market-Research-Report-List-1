<p><p>The retreaded tire market comprises three main types: radial, bias, and solid tires. Radial tires feature steel belts and provide better fuel efficiency and longevity, making them popular for passenger vehicles. Bias tires, characterized by their diagonal ply construction, offer enhanced stability for heavy loads, often used in commercial vehicles. Solid tires are puncture-proof and designed for industrial applications, providing durability on rough surfaces. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying consumer needs across transportation sectors.</p></p>
