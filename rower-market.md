<p><p>The rower market features four primary types: air rowers, magnetic rowers, hydraulic rowers, and water rowers. Air rowers use fan resistance, providing a smooth, dynamic workout that intensifies with speed. Magnetic rowers offer adjustable resistance through magnets for a quieter operation. Hydraulic rowers employ pistons for compact design and variable resistance, ideal for smaller spaces. Water rowers simulate real rowing by utilizing water for resistance, delivering a unique feel and aesthetic appeal. Each type caters to different user preferences and space constraints.</p></p>
