<p><strong>The Sedan Vehicle Thermostat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insert Thermostat</li><li>Housing Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sedan vehicle thermostat market can be divided into two main types: insert thermostats and housing thermostats. Insert thermostats are standalone components that directly regulate coolant temperature, ensuring optimal engine performance. They are typically installed within the coolant flow. In contrast, housing thermostats integrate the thermostat within a housing unit, providing a streamlined design for easier installation. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining efficient engine temperatures, enhancing fuel efficiency, and reducing emissions in sedan vehicles.</p></p>
