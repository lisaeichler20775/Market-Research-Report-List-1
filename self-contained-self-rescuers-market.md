<p><p>The Self-Contained Self-Rescuer (SCSR) market is divided into two main types: Compressed Oxygen SCSRs and Chemical Oxygen SCSRs. Compressed Oxygen SCSRs utilize stored oxygen in high-pressure cylinders, providing a reliable air supply for extended durations. In contrast, Chemical Oxygen SCSRs generate oxygen through chemical reactions, offering a lightweight alternative with potentially longer shelf life. Both types are crucial in emergency situations, particularly in mining and industrial applications, ensuring safety and survival in hazardous environments.</p></p>
