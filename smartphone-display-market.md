<p><p>The smartphone display market consists primarily of rigid and flexible displays. Rigid displays, typically made from glass or hard materials, are widely used due to their durability and cost-effectiveness. They offer high resolution and clarity but are limited in design flexibility. On the other hand, flexible displays, often made from OLED technology, enable innovative designs such as curved or foldable smartphones. These displays are lightweight and can withstand bending, catering to the growing demand for unique form factors in mobile devices.</p></p>
