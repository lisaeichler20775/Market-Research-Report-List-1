<p><p>The SMT SPI (Solder Paste Inspection) equipment market is categorized into inline and offline types. Inline SPI systems integrate directly into the PCB production line, enabling real-time inspection and immediate feedback, which enhances efficiency and reduces defects. In contrast, offline SPI systems operate independently, allowing for batch testing of PCBs outside the production line. While inline systems are favored for continuous production environments, offline systems offer flexibility and detailed analysis, catering to different manufacturing needs and optimizing quality control.</p></p>
