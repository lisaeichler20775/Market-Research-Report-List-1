<p><p>The solar texturing equipment market is primarily divided into two types: monocrystal and polycrystal texturing equipment. Monocrystal texturing equipment is designed for processing monocrystalline silicon wafers, enhancing their surface to improve light absorption and efficiency in solar cells. In contrast, polycrystal texturing equipment targets polycrystalline silicon wafers, optimizing their surface structure to boost performance. Both types play a crucial role in the production of high-efficiency solar panels, catering to increasing demand for renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
