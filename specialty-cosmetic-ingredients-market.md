<p><p>The Specialty Cosmetic Ingredients Market includes various types of functional ingredients. **Emollients** soften and smooth skin by forming a protective barrier. **Surfactants** cleanse and foam, categorized into **Anionics** (negatively charged, strong cleansing) and **Non-Anionics** (mild, non-ionic). **Cationics** (positively charged) condition and improve hair/skin feel. **Amphoterics** (dual charge) are gentle, used in sensitive formulations. **Others** include humectants, preservatives, and active agents. These ingredients cater to diverse cosmetic needs, enhancing texture, performance, and sensory appeal in skincare, haircare, and personal care products.</p></p>
