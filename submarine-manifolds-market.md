<p><p>Submarine manifolds are essential components in offshore oil and gas production, facilitating the control and distribution of fluids. The market includes three primary types: Template Manifolds, which are fixed structures providing support for subsea installations; Cluster Manifolds, designed to manage multiple wells from a single location, optimizing space and efficiency; and Pipeline End Manifolds (PLEMs), used to connect pipelines to subsea systems, ensuring fluid transport. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing production and operational safety.</p></p>
