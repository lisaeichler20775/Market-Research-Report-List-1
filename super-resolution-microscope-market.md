<p><p>The Super-Resolution Microscope market includes several advanced imaging techniques enhancing optical resolution beyond conventional limits. Stimulated Emission Depletion Microscopy (STED) uses depletion of fluorescent states for precise localization. Structured-Illumination Microscopy (SIM) enhances resolution by projecting patterns onto samples. Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy (STORM) employs single-molecule localization for high-resolution images. Fluorescence Photoactivation Localization Microscopy (FPALM) and Photoactivated Localization Microscopy (PALM) utilize photoactivatable fluorophores for imaging, allowing scientists to visualize cellular structures with remarkable clarity and detail.</p></p>
