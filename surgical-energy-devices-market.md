<p><p>The surgical energy devices market encompasses various technologies that enhance surgical precision and efficiency. Monopolar devices use a single electrode to deliver energy, effective for cutting and coagulating tissue. Bipolar devices utilize two electrodes for focused energy delivery, minimizing collateral damage. Ultrasonic devices employ high-frequency sound waves to emulsify tissues, ideal for delicate procedures. Additionally, “Other” devices include laser and radiofrequency tools, each offering unique advantages tailored to specific surgical needs. Together, these technologies improve patient outcomes and streamline surgical processes.</p></p>
