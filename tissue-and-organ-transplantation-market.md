<p><p>The tissue and organ transplantation market comprises three main segments: tissue products, preservation solutions, and immunosuppressive drugs. Tissue products include grafts and implants essential for replacing damaged tissues. Preservation solutions are critical for keeping harvested organs viable during transport and storage, ensuring their functionality post-transplant. Immunosuppressive drugs are vital for preventing organ rejection in transplant recipients, allowing for successful integration of the transplanted tissue or organ into the recipient's body by suppressing the immune response.</p></p>
