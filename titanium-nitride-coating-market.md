<p><p>Titanium Nitride (TiN) coating is a durable, wear-resistant material applied to surfaces to enhance hardness, corrosion resistance, and aesthetic appeal. The market is segmented by coating methods: **Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)** and **Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD)**. CVD involves chemical reactions at high temperatures to deposit TiN, ideal for complex shapes and high-volume production. PVD uses physical processes like sputtering or evaporation at lower temperatures, offering precise, high-quality coatings for tools, medical devices, and decorative finishes. Both methods cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
