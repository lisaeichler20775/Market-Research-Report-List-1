<p><p>The UVC LED market is categorized into three main types: TO (Transistor Outline), SMD (Surface Mount Device), and Others. TO UVC LEDs feature a robust package suitable for high-power applications, providing superior thermal management. SMD UVC LEDs are compact and easily integrated into printed circuit boards, making them ideal for low-profile applications. The "Others" category includes diverse packaging formats and specialized designs, catering to various industrial and consumer needs. Together, these types support the growing demand for UVC germicidal solutions.</p></p>
