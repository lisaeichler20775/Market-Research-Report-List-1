<p><p>Vacuum drum dryers are widely used in various industries for drying heat-sensitive materials. The market includes two primary types: single drum and double drum dryers. Single drum dryers feature one rotating drum, offering simplicity and efficiency for smaller batches. In contrast, double drum dryers consist of two adjacent drums, facilitating higher throughput and uniform drying for larger volumes. Both types utilize reduced pressure to minimize thermal degradation of products, making them ideal for pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemical industries.</p></p>
