<p><ul><li>Receiving Antennas</li><li>Transmitting Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Very Low Frequency (VLF) antennas market is categorized into receiving and transmitting antennas. Receiving antennas are designed to capture VLF signals, typically used in applications like maritime communication and weather data transmission. They are characterized by low gain but high sensitivity to distant signals. Transmitting antennas, on the other hand, are used to send VLF signals, primarily for long-range communication, navigation, and time synchronization. Both types play crucial roles in efficient radio communication across vast distances.</p></p>
