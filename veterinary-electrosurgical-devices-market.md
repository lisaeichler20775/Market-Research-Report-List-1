<p><strong>The Veterinary Electrosurgical Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Electrosurgical Devices</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary electrosurgical devices market includes two primary types: monopolar and bipolar electrosurgical devices. Monopolar devices utilize a single electrode, allowing current to flow from the surgical site through the patient to the grounding pad, making them suitable for various procedures. In contrast, bipolar devices have two electrodes that deliver current directly through the tissue, improving precision and minimizing surrounding tissue damage. Both types enhance surgical efficiency, reduce hemorrhage, and promote faster recovery in veterinary patients.</p></p>
