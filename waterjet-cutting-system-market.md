<p><p>The waterjet cutting system market comprises two primary types: abrasive waterjet and pure waterjet. Abrasive waterjet systems utilize a mixture of high-pressure water and abrasive materials, making them suitable for cutting hard materials like metal and stone with precision. In contrast, pure waterjet systems rely solely on high-pressure water jets, effectively cutting softer materials such as paper, rubber, and food products. Both technologies offer unique advantages in various industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, and architecture, enhancing cutting efficiency and versatility.</p></p>
