<p><strong>The Waterproof Breathable Textiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Densely Woven</li><li>Coated and Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof breathable textiles market consists of three primary types: densely woven fabrics, coated materials, and membrane technologies. Densely woven fabrics utilize tightly woven fibers to create a barrier against water while allowing moisture vapor to escape. Coated textiles have a polymer layer applied to the fabric, providing waterproofing while maintaining breathability. Membrane textiles feature a thin film laminated to the fabric, which is engineered to be both waterproof and breathable, offering enhanced performance for various applications, including outdoor apparel and gear.</p></p>
